The task in this project is to analyse the similarities and differences between data science departments using unsupervised learning techniques.

Backend:

The modelling team should direct their findings to University administrators who would want to use the results to identify gaps/strengths in their program for future improvements. University administrators are typically former academics, so they are usually comfortable with technical details. However they are extremely busy.
